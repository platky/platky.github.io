HGGodWars


Information

HGGodWars stands for Heroes Gathering God Wars and is a custom built Minecraft plugin for a custom server I was building, managing and running. The plugin was comprehensive in planned and existing features. The basis was to devote yourself to gods that are user defined and created. The more people under one god the better.

Each player generates "karma" for their god. If your god has the most points at the start of every hour you get a major buff depending on the class of your god. This could be something like health increases, breathing underwater and more. Karma is generated through quests, good or bad deeds, killing and more. If your god is aligned evil then things like Player Vs Player killing is valued and vice versa.

Tools

Minecraft is a Java based game and as such the plugins are also built in Java. HGGodWars is built for an older version of Minecraft (1.7) on a suite that no longer exists (Bukkit). It was design and implemented in Eclipse using Maven for compiling.
